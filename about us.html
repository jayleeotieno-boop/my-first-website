Who are we

Hey there! We're just regular fans who think F1 is way too fun to stay confusing. This site was born because we remember how overwhelming it felt at first—all those rules, weird words like "porpoising," and fans arguing about tire strategies. So we kept it simple. No fancy jargon. No gatekeeping. Just the good stuff explained like we're talking to a friend. Whether you binged *Drive to Survive* or just saw a cool crash clip online, you're welcome here. Grab a coffee, poke around, and let's get you race-ready.
